Maji_Ndogo_Water_Crisis_Project

The project aims at solving the water problem in a fictitious country known as Maji Ndogo. It has been broken down into part and the they serve the following purposes:

Part 1:
We've got this mountain of data, and it's our job to sift through it, finding those nuggets of insight that will help us solve our water crisis here in Maji Ndogo. 

Part 2:
Illuminates the road ahead, magnifies our analysis, moving beyond isolated data points to discern larger patterns and trends. We cluster our data, stepping back from the individual figures to gain a panoramic understanding. This bird's eye view will allow us to unearth broader narratives and hidden correlations concealed within our rich dataset. 

Part 3:
Sets the stage for our exploratory data analysis. 

Part 4:
Provides essential information to decision-makers. This will enable us to discern the materials we need (to repair our water infrastructure), plan our budgets, and identify the areas requiring immediate attention.
